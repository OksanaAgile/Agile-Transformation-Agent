Case Studies: Quantifiable Transformation Results

See how strategic coaching, tooling optimization, and mindset shifts have led to massive improvements in flow, alignment, and time-to-market.

Case 1: 40% Reduction in Time-to-Market

Challenge: Slow, siloed delivery for a global product line using waterfall methods.

Solution: Implemented a Scaled Agile Framework (SAFe/LeSS hybrid) across 15 teams, coaching 5 Release Train Engineers and 10 Product Owners.

**Result:** Reduced end-to-end delivery cycle from 6 months to 3.6 months. Full details available on GitHub.

Case 2: 50 Point Increase in Organizational Flow Efficiency

Challenge: High WIP and poor predictability in a legacy R&D department.

Solution: Introduced advanced Kanban methods, limiting WIP, and focusing on Value Stream mapping to eliminate bottlenecks.

**Result:** Flow efficiency (touch time/lead time) improved from 15% to 65%.
